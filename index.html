Shopify Developer

Shopify is the solution recommended by my friends at Baron Fig when I was researching an ecommerce solution for Wineries of the Sierra Foothills. It's a hosted solution that gives you extraordinary control over the both the look and functionality of your site. With their templating language, you have complete control over the design of the site. Their API and webhooks allow you integrate with a variety of third-party services and let you build your own. You get all the benefits of a hosted solution plus the control of having your own hosted site. Check out my demo shop in progress using Bootstrap as the base.
